Meet the Duo

Tonimarie Morris and Larry Kent are 4th Street Manor, a duo that specializes in songs of love, life, joy, heartbreak, and longing. Their diverse repertoire includes songs by Beth Hart, Bonnie Raitt, Duran Duran, Bob Dylan, The Civil Wars, KT Tunstall, Melissa Etheridge, and many others, plus originals written by and for the duo.

Tonimarie Morris (vocalist) is also lead vocalist for Blues Soup, one of Central Virginia's top classic rock cover bands. She has also sung with Lost Dog, in addition to appearing as a guest with numerous bands in Virginia and West Virginia over the last 15 years.

Larry Kent (keys, guitar, vocals) also plays for Broken Angel, a popular rock and country cover band that performs throughout Virginia. He also plays keys and sings with Gabby Haze, an Americana band that performs classic rock, folk-rock and original songs throughout Virginia.
